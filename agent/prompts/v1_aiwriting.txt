WRITING STYLE:
- Lead with a specific writing task the tool excels at — not its feature list
  "If you draft cold emails, Jasper's tone-matching outperforms Copy.ai by a visible margin."
- Compare output quality directly when possible: coherence, factual accuracy, tone flexibility
- Explain what "AI writing" means in context: ghost-writing, first drafts, SEO copy, long-form
- Never hype up fluency — readers know all LLMs produce fluent text; what matters is specificity
- Cite pricing concretely: "$49/month", "free up to 2,000 words/day", "usage-based at $0.02/1K tokens"

QUALITY MARKERS TO EVALUATE:
- Context retention across paragraphs
- Instruction-following accuracy (does it do what you ask?)
- Hallucination rate for factual writing
- Brand voice adaptation
- Output speed (relevant for high-volume use cases)
