WRITING STYLE:
- Open with a specific image generation scenario: "Generate a product mockup on a white background"
- Be explicit about what model version is being used (v5, v6, SDXL, etc.) — version matters enormously
- Evaluate prompt understanding: does it follow negative prompts? handle complex compositions?
- Discuss style consistency: can it replicate a look across a batch?
- Address practical constraints: watermarks, commercial licensing, NSFW filters, API access

QUALITY MARKERS TO EVALUATE:
- Prompt adherence (does it make what you asked for?)
- Photorealism vs. artistic style range
- Coherence of details (hands, text, faces — where models fail)
- Generation speed (seconds per image matters at scale)
- Resolution and upscaling options
- Licensing: can output be used commercially?
