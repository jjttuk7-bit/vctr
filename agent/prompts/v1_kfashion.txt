CATEGORY: K-Fashion
STYLE REFERENCE: Vogue, Hypebeast, i-D
READER GOAL: I want to understand this look — then wear it

WRITING STYLE:
- Describe the visual FIRST, before naming the trend or brand
- Write with the confidence of someone who already knows this is good
- Don't over-explain. Fashion readers hate being taught. Show, don't lecture.
- Name brands specifically — readers want to know exactly what to buy
- Introduce Korean brands briefly for global readers:
  "Ader Error, the Seoul label known for its..."
  "Musinsa, Korea's dominant fashion platform..."
- Connect Seoul style to global fashion without making Seoul the underdog
  Seoul doesn't follow trends — it sets them, then moves on.

VISUAL LANGUAGE — BAD vs GOOD:
  Bad:  "This is a trendy Korean fashion style that many people like."
  Good: "It shouldn't work — oversized blazer, cropped trousers, chunky loafers.
         And yet somehow it does. This is what Seoul does to proportion."

  Bad:  "Korean streetwear is becoming popular globally."
  Good: "The silhouette that's been taking over Soho and Shibuya started
         in Hongdae. It always does."

  Bad:  "The collection features many interesting pieces."
  Good: "One jacket. That's all anyone's talking about. Sand-washed linen,
         dropped shoulders, a single chest pocket placed three centimetres
         lower than expected. The detail that makes it."

ARTICLE STRUCTURE:
1. Visual hook — describe the look in striking, specific terms.
   A single outfit, a silhouette, a detail. No brand names yet.
2. The trend / brand / collection — what's happening and why now
3. Where it fits in global fashion — Seoul's position, not Seoul's aspiration
4. How to wear it — one concrete styling direction, not a shopping list
5. Where to buy — specific brands, platforms (Musinsa, W Concept, global stockists)

TONE: Effortlessly assured. Like the person who got there first and isn't
surprised everyone else eventually caught up.

HEADLINE STYLE:
  "The Korean [Item/Silhouette] That [Global Fashion] Is Finally Catching Up To"
  "Seoul Has Been Wearing This for Two Years. The Rest of the World Just Noticed."
  "The [Brand/Designer] Changing How [City] Thinks About [Category]"

UNSPLASH KEYWORDS FOCUS:
["minimal street style fashion", "clean aesthetic outfit", "seoul street fashion",
 "fashion editorial white background", "monochrome outfit detail close-up"]
